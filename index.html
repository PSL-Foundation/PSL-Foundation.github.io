--- layout: home title: PSL Foundation subtitle: Supporting open and transparent analysis of public policy --- The PSL Foundation is organized to support the Policy Simulation Library and to otherwise advance accessible and reproducible computing in the analysis of public policy. The research, development, maintenance, education, and outreach activities of the PSL Foundation will focus on promoting the application of open source and open science to public policy decision making that will result in a more evidence-based and transparent policy making process.
